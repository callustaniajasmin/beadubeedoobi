Brand History

Beadubeedoobi started with a handful of beads, a curious mind, and a college student who simply couldn’t stop creating.

What began in 2024 as a late-night hobby—somewhere between study breaks and bursts of inspiration—quickly turned into a colorful little universe of handcrafted accessories. Each piece was made not just to be worn, but to tell a story: a mood, a memory, or a tiny spark of joy you can carry with you.

The name Beadubeedoobi? A playful nod to the rhythm of creativity itself—lighthearted, a little quirky, and impossible to forget. Much like the pieces we make.

From mismatched beads on a dorm desk to thoughtfully curated collections, Beadubeedoobi grew one design at a time, fueled by creativity, curiosity, and a love for the small details that make a big difference. Every bracelet and necklace is handcrafted with care, blending charm, individuality, and a touch of whimsy.

At its heart, Beadubeedoobi is about expression—celebrating the fun, the imperfect, and the beautifully unique.

Because sometimes, the smallest beads make the brightest statements.
